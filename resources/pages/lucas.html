ineffa lucas

Ineffa Lucas is one of, if not the biggest name as a South Korean artist in the New Zealand music industry. From founding New Zealand’s first ever South Korean hip hop music group, Ineffa Crew in 2017 to performing alongside legend rapper, MC Meta in front of hundreds, to releasing his first official album ‘ROSE’ in 2020 featuring big names such as MckDaddy, Donutman and Ash-B, Ineffa Lucas is definitely one of the pioneers of the music scene in the country.
